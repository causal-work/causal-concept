What is Causal?
Causal connects individual actions to strategic leverage points in economic and political systems. The platform makes coordination visible, surfaces optimal timing for pressure campaigns, and grounds strategy in Earth4All's validated systems science.

Why Coordination Matters
1,000 people acting at the same leverage point creates exponentially more pressure than 10,000 scattered across time and targets.
Example: 5,000 people submitting comments this week signals organized pressure to policymakers. Same people spread over 3 months = background noise.
