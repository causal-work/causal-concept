Systems Analysis Tools

Stock-Flow Mapper
Visualize how systems accumulate (stocks) and change over time (flows). Identify where to "stop the flow" versus mopping up accumulated problems.
Example: Model fossil fuel infrastructure lock-in‚Äî40 years of emissions from each project approved today
Integration: Embed Insight Maker (free, open-source) for browser-based stock-flow modeling
Intervention Scenario Modeler
Test different coordination strategies before execution. Compare transformation pathways and model how interventions cascade through connected systems.
Example: Compare top-down vs bottom-up vs hybrid approaches for Just Transition Fund
Development: Custom scenario comparison UI on Insight Maker simulation engine
Tipping Point Analysis
Identify when multiple factors converge to create transformation windows. Analyze coordination timing for maximum leverage effect.
Example: Detect when public pressure + insider support + crisis align for policy transformation
Development: Custom dashboard aggregating Watch data, coordination metrics, external signals
Cross-Boundary Assessment
Ensure proposed policies don't optimize one planetary boundary while breaching others. Map interventions across Earth4All turnarounds.
Example: Verify biofuel policy doesn't breach land-use or biodiversity boundaries via deforestation
Development: Custom visualization of Stockholm Resilience Centre's planetary boundaries framework
Feedback Loop Visualizer
Map reinforcing and balancing loops in complex systems. Understand how interventions create cascade effects across sectors.
Example: Map how worker economic fear creates reinforcing loop blocking energy transition
Integration: Embed LOOPY (simple causal loops) or Kumu (complex network mapping)
Scenario Library
Browse tested intervention scenarios from other initiatives. Adapt proven coordination strategies to your context.
